About Seeds of Change

Seeds of Change is a grassroots climate education initiative co-founded by TracyAnn Wacuka and a team of passionate educators and youth leaders. We work with schools in Nairobi and beyond to integrate climate literacy into early childhood and primary education.

Our vision is to nurture environmentally responsible learners and equip teachers with tools to deliver engaging and contextually relevant climate education content.

Our Programs

Planet Protectors Club

A fun, learner-led climate club where children aged 10–16 explore environmental topics through games, storytelling, and action projects.

Teacher Training Workshops

We train teachers on how to use climate-focused lesson plans and interactive pedagogy based on our “Securing Our Future” manual.

Local Climate Learning Resources

We co-create localized, child-friendly materials like posters, songs, and activity books that speak to the environmental realities of Kenyan learners.

Our Impact

Since launching, Seeds of Change has reached over 300 learners across Parklands, Dandora, Nairobi West, and Heidi Marie schools. Teachers trained through our workshops report increased confidence and capacity to teach climate topics, and learners have begun initiating tree planting, waste sorting, and peer education efforts.
